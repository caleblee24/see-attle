    Seattle Meowtropolitan

    Come stop by Seattle Meowtropolitan to play with cats while sipping on some coffee and snacking on some pastries. Reservations are recommended but walk-ins are welcomed.

    Reviews

    Thanh Ly

    03/07/2023

    I came with my friend and we had made a reservation before hand. We arrived about 10 minutes early and decided to get some coffee. I got a 12oz latte and was very surprised at the cute cat latte art the barista had made! My friend got an americano, so no latte art for him. About 5 minutes before our reservation, one of the staff came out and gave us a rundown on some rules to make sure we can enjoy our time with the cats to the fullest. The cats were adorable and really liked my friend, he had 3 of them playing with him at one point. We would totally come by again!

    Nathan Louie

    03/07/2023

    Super cute cats. I thought I was going to get bored midway but time passed by faster than I thought it would.
